                                                                      Crafting Spells

                                                                      Deceptively tricky. This spell has nearly driven me mad trying to figure it out. It behaves stupidly simple yet preparing the conditions for the spell to work can become a chore. I placed my materials for a chain, one iron ingot in the center and two iron nuggets at opposite sides. And it worked, no matter the ordering in my list.
